DungeonBreaker

You, an adventurer, travel across the land to dungeons to explore. Having come to a dungeon, you have to decide what four items to take with you into the dungeon, if you don't pick the right ones, and the order to use them in, it can spell doom. Luckily you have a magic shield with you that can help, you place four items infront of the shield in the order you think will help and the shield will give you hints. However, you only have ten charges on the shield, if you don't guess correctly you'll have to enter the dungeon unawares.
When you place an four items in a row next to the shield it will produce four lights, green lights means that you have a correct item in the correct spot. Yellow means that you have a correct item but in the incorrect spot. And black means that you have an incorrect item.
